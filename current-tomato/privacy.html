Privacy Policy for Current Tomato

Effective Date: 02/12/2025

This privacy policy applies specifically to the Current Tomato app developed by Rough Idea Software.

Current Tomato (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our app.

  1. Information Collection

    Current Tomato does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app functions entirely on your Apple Watch and operates locally.

  2. Usage Data

    The app does not track or transmit any usage data, analytics, or personal activity.

  3. Notifications

    The app uses haptic notifications to alert you when a timer completes. These notifications are generated locally on your device and are not shared or sent to any third party.

  4. Third-Party Services

    Current Tomato does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

  5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here.
